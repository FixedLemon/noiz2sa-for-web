Noiz2sa for Web

English / Japanese

Bullet Hell Shooter: Noiz2sa

This game is a web browser port of the bullet hell shooter “Noiz2sa,” created by Kenta Cho.

You can play it not only on a PC browser but also on smartphones and tablets.
When your device is in portrait orientation, the display layout will be optimized for vertical screens.

Start the Game

How to Play

Control your ship and dodge the bullet barrage.

Your ship will not be destroyed if it comes into contact with enemy bodies.
While holding down the “Slow Down” button, your ship will move more slowly.

Green stars are bonus items.
Collecting them consecutively increases the points awarded for the item.

If all your ships are destroyed, it’s game over.
You gain one additional ship for every 200,000 and 500,000 points scored.
